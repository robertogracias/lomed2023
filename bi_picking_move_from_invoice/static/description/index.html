Odoo Stock Move And Picking From Invoice and Vendor bill Odoo apps

In Odoo this app automate warehouse inventory movement of stock move and stock picking from customer invoice or vendor bills. This will allow Update stock automatically when validate Invoice in Odoo.

Normally we can create Picking and Stock move when we confirm sale order in odoo but default odoo does not provide such functionality to create stock moves and picking directly from invoice.

In major cases with some businesses provide direct invoices to their customers so they cannot maintain stock. It can save your time if stock moves and picking get created automatically with invoices.

To make that use-cases simple we have added this module to make work fast and simple.

Features

Create stock picking from Invoice/Vendor bill

Have option to create stock picking or stock move from the invoice.

Smart button showing stock move/picking at same time

User can redirect to related stock move/picking from invoice throught smart buttons.

Create stock move from Invoice/Vendor bill

Able to create picking from the customer invoice as well as from vendor bills.

Auto selected picking type.

When you make picking/move from the customer invoice it will generated delivery order/moves and when you make picking/move from the vendor bills its generated incoming shipments(moves).
